This complete Low Poly Chess Asset Pack (and growing) contains a wide variety of  game assets which has been textured, unwrapped, and UV mapped.The asset can be easily imported into Unity. They can be used for non-commercial and commercial games

3D Model Specifications
Geometry: Polygon
Textures: Yes
Materials: Yes
UV Mapped: Yes
Unwrapped UVs: Yes
Formats - .FBX, and .OBJ
Texture Format: PNG
Texture Size: 256x256 
UV Information in Texture: Yes
Maps: Diffuse (Color)
Mobile Ready: Yes
Tried and Tested in: Unity 5+
