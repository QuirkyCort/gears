Three rigged and animated low poly aquatic animals. You can download both the .fbx and .blend versions of the files as a .zip, or you can download and import a unity package (which includes basic animator controllers).

This package contains 

- A woolly mammoth, with idle, walk, and head shake animations

- A saber-tooth tiger, with idle, walk, run, and bite animations

- A giant sloth, with idle and walk animations
